Welcome to the Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce. Our purpose is to create a strong network of businesses within our community. The Chamber of Commerce consists of hundreds of businesses in southeastern idaho. From small family owned businesses to large corporations, our organization conncets the community and strengthens the local economy.

Upcoming Events

On December 15th there will be Christmas parade at Freeman Park sponsered by local doctor's offices. All are welcome to attend. To register a float or vehicle please call Janet Smith at 208.867.5309. During the parade local Doctors will be collecting donations to go towards getting Christmas presents for families who are in need.
On December 24th a Christmas Tree lighting will take place at McCowin Park. It will be sponsered by some of our local banks! Over 30 very large trees will be lit up to bring in Christmas to southeastern Idaho. Lighting will start at 6:00pm. Hot chocolate will be served.
Help us bring in the new year by celebrating 2022 with us at the Civic Auditorium. There will be several live performaces by local bands and a surpise keynote speaker! This event is sponsered by local insurance compaines. Tickets are on sale now starting at $12.
